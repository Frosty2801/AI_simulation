# PetCare Medellin Policies

## Appointment Policy

- Appointments are subject to schedule availability.
- Owners should arrive 10 minutes before the scheduled time when possible.
- Late arrivals may require rescheduling if they affect the next appointments.
- The clinic may request confirmation on the day of service.

## Cancellation and Rescheduling Policy

- Cancellations should be reported as early as possible.
- Rescheduling requests are processed according to available slots.
- Repeated no-shows may require advance confirmation or deposit for future appointments.

## Emergency Policy

- Messages classified as urgent are prioritized over routine requests.
- The automated assistant must not delay escalation if the message suggests life-threatening symptoms.
- Emergency availability depends on veterinarian capacity, but urgent cases must still be directed immediately.

## Pricing Policy

- Published prices are reference values unless otherwise stated as fixed.
- Surgical, hospitalization, imaging, and emergency costs may vary after evaluation.
- The clinic may update prices without prior notice.

## Payment Policy

- Payment is generally due at the time of consultation, procedure, or discharge unless another arrangement is approved.
- Some services may require prepayment, partial deposit, or prior authorization.
- Outstanding balances may delay release of non-urgent documents.

## Records and Follow-Up Policy

- Follow-up requests should include enough detail to identify the patient and previous visit.
- Medical information is only released to authorized owners or approved contacts.
- Response time for non-urgent follow-up may depend on veterinarian availability.

## Surgical Policy

- Surgical procedures may require fasting, signed consent, and pre-surgical tests.
- The clinic may postpone surgery if the patient is not medically stable.
- Owners must follow discharge instructions and report warning signs immediately.

## Medication Policy

- The clinic does not recommend self-medicating pets with human drugs.
- Prescriptions and treatment instructions must be confirmed by a veterinarian.
- Refill approval depends on the patient's condition and previous evaluation.

## Communication Policy

- Incoming requests from Telegram, email, and web forms are processed according to urgency and category.
- Administrative questions may receive automated replies when the answer is defined in clinic documentation.
- Clinical answers generated by the assistant should be based only on approved business documents.

## Data and Privacy Policy

- Owner contact information is used for scheduling, follow-up, and service communications.
- Access to medical details should be limited to authorized personnel and validated owners.
- Sensitive data should not be exposed in public channels or unsecured exports.

## Refund Policy

- Administrative fees already consumed in a completed service are generally non-refundable.
- Deposits for procedures may be refundable only under the conditions defined by the clinic.
- Refund exceptions require administrative review.

## Automation Rules for Policy Retrieval

- Questions about deposits, no-shows, payments, hours, address, records, and certificates usually map to ADMINISTRATIVA.
- Questions about discharge rules or post-surgical instructions may map to CONSULTA or SEGUIMIENTO depending on context.
