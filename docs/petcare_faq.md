# PetCare Medellin FAQ

## General Information

### What are the clinic hours?

PetCare Medellin operates Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Sunday attention is limited and depends on emergency availability.

### Where is the clinic located?

PetCare Medellin serves clients in Medellin. The exact address can be shared through the administrative response template configured by the clinic.

### Do you attend emergencies?

Yes. Emergency attention depends on clinical severity, veterinarian availability, and current capacity. If the pet has breathing difficulty, seizures, severe bleeding, poisoning, or collapse, the owner should go immediately to the clinic or nearest emergency center.

## Appointments

### How do I schedule an appointment?

The owner can request an appointment by indicating:
- pet species
- pet name
- main reason for visit
- preferred date
- contact number

### Can I reschedule an appointment?

Yes. Rescheduling is subject to availability. The owner should provide the existing appointment data and the new preferred slot.

### Can I cancel an appointment?

Yes. The clinic recommends canceling as early as possible so the slot can be reassigned.

## Vaccination

### What information do I need for a vaccine appointment?

Bring the previous vaccination card if available and inform the clinic if the pet has vomiting, diarrhea, fever, or current medication.

### Can a sick pet be vaccinated?

Not always. A veterinarian must evaluate the pet if there are active symptoms before deciding whether vaccination should proceed.

## Surgery

### What should I know before sterilization?

The clinic may provide fasting instructions, request pre-surgical evaluation, and ask about medications or prior disease history.

### How long is recovery after sterilization?

Recovery varies by patient, but the owner should follow medication instructions, protect the wound, and report swelling, discharge, bleeding, or unusual lethargy.

## Payments

### What payment methods are accepted?

Payment methods may include cash, transfer, debit card, and credit card depending on clinic policy and POS availability.

### Do you require advance payment?

Some procedures or bookings may require partial advance payment or confirmation deposit.

## Follow-Up

### How do I ask about my pet's ongoing case?

The owner should provide:
- pet name
- owner name
- date of last visit
- short summary of the case or procedure

This helps the clinic classify the request as SEGUIMIENTO and review the correct record.

### What if my pet gets worse after treatment?

If there is vomiting, bleeding, breathing difficulty, fainting, wound opening, or rapid worsening, the owner should seek urgent attention immediately.

## Documents and Certificates

### Can I request a vaccination certificate?

Yes. Certificate availability depends on completed records in the clinic system.

### Can I request a clinical summary?

Yes. Some summaries may require processing time and verification by the attending veterinarian.

## Grooming and Non-Clinical Questions

### Do you offer bathing or grooming?

Availability depends on the clinic's current service configuration. The automated assistant can inform the owner whether the service is available and how to request it.

## FAQ Routing Hints

- Schedule, reschedule, and cancel requests usually map to AGENDAMIENTO.
- Hours, location, payments, and certificates usually map to ADMINISTRATIVA.
- Ongoing case status usually maps to SEGUIMIENTO.
- Questions about vaccines, deworming, surgery, and symptoms without obvious danger usually map to CONSULTA.
