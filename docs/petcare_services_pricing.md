# PetCare Medellin Services and Pricing

## Purpose

This document contains the reference catalog of standard services and estimated prices used for automated responses. Final prices may vary depending on medical evaluation, pet size, species, required supplies, and additional tests.

## General Consultation

### Initial General Consultation

- Service code: PC-GEN-001
- Estimated price: COP 70,000
- Includes: basic clinical evaluation, symptom review, and treatment orientation
- Does not include: medication, laboratory tests, imaging, procedures

### Follow-Up Consultation

- Service code: PC-GEN-002
- Estimated price: COP 50,000
- Applies when scheduled as follow-up after previous consultation within the period defined by the treating veterinarian

## Commercial Rules for Automated Responses

- Automated assistants must present prices as estimated reference values.
- Automated assistants must not promise a final price without veterinary review when a range is defined.
- If the user asks for an exact surgery cost, the system should explain that the final quote depends on evaluation, size, species, and required tests.
- If the message is only asking for costs, the likely classification is CONSULTA or ADMINISTRATIVA depending on wording.
